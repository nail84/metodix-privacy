Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how FitTrainer ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account or sign in with Google, we collect your email address and display name.

Workout Data: The App stores data you voluntarily enter, including:

  • Training programs and exercises
  • Workout sessions and sets (weights, reps, duration)
  • Client profiles (if you use the trainer features)
  • Progress notes

Automatically Collected: We do not collect device identifiers, location data, or analytics beyond what is necessary for authentication.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • To store and display your workout history and progress
  • To enable trainer-client relationships within the App

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase cloud infrastructure with row-level security policies ensuring that each user can only access their own data. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (Supabase Privacy Policy)
  • Google Sign-In — optional authentication method (Google Privacy Policy)

We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics trackers, or any other third-party services that collect user data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Your workout data is private and accessible only to you (and your trainer/clients if you use group features).

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. Upon request, all your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
